Blooming Roads, Safe Travels: Springtime with the Highway Patrol

Spring has sprung, bringing sunshine, blooming flowers, and...increased traffic! As nature awakens, so does the need for extra vigilance on the roads. The Highway Patrol is here to ensure your springtime journeys are smooth and safe.

  • Be mindful of changing weather conditions. Spring showers can create slippery roads and reduced visibility.
  • Watch out for wildlife. Deer and other animals are more active during this season, so stay alert.
  • Plan your trips ahead of time. Allow extra travel time to avoid stress and rushing.
  • Check your vehicle before hitting the road. Ensure proper tire pressure, working lights, and wipers.
